Security Audits (SA)

Security audit is essentially an assessment of how effectively the organization's security policy is being implemented. This assumes that the organization has a security policy in place which, unfortunately, is not always the case.

Even today, it is possible to find a number of organizations where a written security policy does not exist. Security policies are a means of standardizing security practices by having them codified (in writing) and agreed to by employees who read them and sign off on them.

When security practices are unwritten or informal, they may not be generally understood and practiced by all employees in the organization. Furthermore, until all employees have read and signed off on the security policy, compliance of the policy cannot be enforced.
