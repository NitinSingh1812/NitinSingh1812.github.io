Careers

We are a people led organization and this is what we choose to be! Unlike other businesses where brick, mortar and inventory are qualified as major assets of the business. At Om Care Taker, people are the only assets that we possess. We believe that each employee contributes directly to the growth of the company and indirectly, to the brand perception.

Many amongst Om Care Taker’s senior management have risen from the ranks which reflect our commitment to provide for continuous growth & development of our employees.

Everyone at Om Care Taker takes immense pride in being a part of this colossal family and don the Om Care Taker shield badge on their chests with great pride. We are always on the lookout for talented people with a can-do attitude to work with us so do send in your updated CV to us at info@omcaretaker.com

Rewarding Work at Om Care Taker

The Department of Om Care Taker has unique career opportunities that will challenge your mind and reward your skills and talents. As a Om Care Taker employee, you will help secure our borders, airports, seaports and waterways; research and develop the latest security technologies; respond to natural disasters or terrorists acts; and analyze intelligence reports.

Unique Career Opportunities

From general mission support to law enforcement to immigration and travel security to prevention and response, exciting professional growth opportunities exist throughout the Department.

Mission support

Careers involve the following fields: medical, human resources, facilities, budget, procurement, science and technology, training, intelligence, planning and coordination, detection, civil rights, fraud detection and more.

Law enforcement

Careers offer positions in protection of the President, Vice President, their families, heads of state and other designated individuals; securing the nation’s borders and enforcing immigration laws; interagency law enforcement training; and enforcing economic, transportation and infrastructure security.

Prevention and Response

Careers protect the public, environment and security interests in any maritime region, as well as provide preparedness, protection, response, recovery and mitigation to reduce loss of life and property and protect the nation from all hazards are what these careers are all about.
